Sur Tarang

STEP UP

"There are shortcuts to happiness and dancing is one of them." Dance is an art of time and space. Anakhronos brings to you one of the most enthralling events "Step Up", which is a SOLO DANCE COMPETITION. It encourages all those budding artists who are willing to spread out their wings and vibe to that music. We welcome you to feel that spark inside you and fly with us!

2 Mins To Fame

"Your talent is God's gift to you. What you do with it is your gift back to God." P.T Barnum once said that No one ever made a difference by being like everyone else.
